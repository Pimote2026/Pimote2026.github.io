## Before Contacting Us

To resolve common issues quickly, please try the following steps first:

1. Make sure **Pimote is up to date** by checking the App Store.
2. Restart the app and/or your device.
3. If the issue continues, contact us using the information below.

---

## How to Report a Bug

To help us investigate and fix the issue faster, please include as much information as possible:

- **App version** — for example, `1.0`. You can usually find it in Settings → Pimote.
- **Device and iOS version** — for example, `iPhone 14, iOS 20.x`.
- **Steps to reproduce the issue** — describe what you did step by step.
- **Expected result** — what you expected to happen.
- **Actual result** — what actually happened.
- **Screenshots or screen recording** — if available.
- **Network status** — Wi-Fi, cellular data, VPN, region, if relevant.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### The app crashes when I open it

Please try deleting and reinstalling the app from the App Store.  
If the issue continues, contact us with your iPhone model, iOS version, and Pimote app version.


### I found a bug

Please email us with the details listed in the **How to Report a Bug** section above.  
The more details you provide, the faster we can investigate the issue.


### I have a feature request

We welcome suggestions and feedback.  
Feel free to email us with your idea and a short explanation of how it would improve your experience with Pimote.
